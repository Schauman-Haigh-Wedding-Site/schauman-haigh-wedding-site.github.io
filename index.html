What to wear

It is important that you feel comfortable, so don't worry if the dress code doesn't suit your style. Please come as yourself! However, we encourage the use of full length gowns for a festive atmosphere that will match the formal venue. Also, please note that the use of stiletto heels should be avoided, due to the sensitive flooring at the venue.
